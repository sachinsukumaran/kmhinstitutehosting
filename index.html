About Dr. Kamakshi Institute of Medical Sciences and Research

The Dr. Kamakshi Institute of Medical Sciences & Research (KIMSR) was established in the year 2010 by Dr.T.G.Govindarajan with a vision of generating required human resources for the healthcare industry. It is a unique academic institution that is committed to academic excellence. The institute is an additional facility of Dr. Kamakshi Memorial Hospital Pvt. Ltd., founded in 2005, with a vision “to serve and deliver every patient with the ultimate excellence and devotion, as we care for our loved ones”. The hospital is a 300 bedded Multi Super Specialty hospital at Pallikaranai, situated in the southern part of Chennai, spread over 60 ground land and 75000 square ft building area.

The KIMSR is affiliated with The Tamil Nadu Dr. M. G. R Medical University under the Allied Health Sciences stream. The various courses conducted are B.Sc Radiography and Imaging Technology (RIT), B.Sc Radiotherapy technology (RTT), and B.Sc Medical Laboratory Technology (MLT). The courses are of 4-year duration, including a 3-year course and a one-year internship. The departments are headed by highly experienced teachers with rich clinical experience as well as in technology. The RIT and RTT courses are duly approved by Atomic Energy Regulatory Board, Govt. of India, so that our students can work throughout India. As of 2019, 185 students have graduated, including 118 from Radiography and Imaging Technology, 52 from Radiotherapy technology and 15 from Medical laboratory Technology. All the students have got good placement both in India and abroad and earning several thousand to lakhs INR as a perk.

The KIMSR is also conducting Diploma in National board courses (DNB,2011) on Radiodiagnosis and Family medicine and so far 11 candidates have successfully passed the DNB courses and all are well employed. The institute is also approved by Anna University, Chennai, as an R&D institute, to conduct research on Medical physics speciality. At present, 3 students are presuming their PhD program in the University. In addition, the institute offers implant training, short and long term training programs for Biomedical Engineering, Nursing, Medical physics students of various other institutions and colleges in and around Chennai. In Medical Physics itself, about 48, 35, 8 students of various Universities have completed their implant training, project dissertation and one-year internship, respectively.

Recently, the institute has started M.Sc., Radiology and Imaging Technology course under The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R Medical University, and many more such courses are in the pipeline. Similarly, few more DNB courses will be started shortly on rare specialities.

About Dr.Kamakshi Memorial Group of Hospitals

Vision

To serve, and deliver every patient, ‘the ultimate’ quality of health care service with utmost excellence and devotion, as we care for our loved ones.

Mission and Values:

  1. Commitment and personal responsibility in every patient’s wellness
  2. Inventiveness and furtherance in every aspect of medical services.
  3. Ethical and reliable in every act of work.

Dr. Kamakshi Memorial Hospital is a 300 bedded multi-super speciality hospital at Pallikaranai, Chennai, established in 2005. It is situated in a sprawling 60 grounds land with 75000 Sq.ft building. The guiding principle of the hospital is to offer the latest in health care facilities on Par with international standards (obviating the need to go abroad for treatment) and also make them available to increasing numbers of our population. To meet the stringent demands of quality health care the services of several talented and dedicated medical professionals (both within the country and overseas) has been and a huge investment made in infrastructure and medical equipment.

Apart from its outpatient and daycare service, the hospital caters to all strata of people by offering inpatient services in general wards, non a/c, a/c rooms, deluxe and super deluxe rooms. The hospital has five well-equipped intensive care units, one of them exclusively for cardiac and post-cardiac surgery patients. The hospital has seven major theatres and one minor theatre.

Department of Radiology and Imaging sciences

The department of Radiology and Imaging Sciences is fully equipped with Digital Radiography, Mammography, Bone Mineral Densitometer, Ultra sonogram, Multislice CT scanner and 1.5 Tesla MRI scanner. All interventional procedures are carried out at ease in the department. The cardiac catheterization lab is equipped with the latest GE Optima IGS 320 machine in which all cardiac interventions are carried out.

Department Laboratory Sciences

The NABL accredited laboratory is fully computerized and it is an automated laboratory, equipped to perform 3000 unique investigations spanning across

  • Clinical Biochemistry
  • Pathology
  • Cytology
  • Serology
  • Microbiology
  • Hematology
  • Molecular Biology, and
  • Histopathology.

Transfusion Medicine (Blood bank) deals with the transfusion of compatible blood and its components. It also involves the study of blood groups, blood requirement of patients and thereby providing safe transfusion for needy patients.

Exclusive machine for plasma separation – Aphaeresis is an added feather to the cap of the transfusion department. The department of offers an active round the clock service meeting up to the NACO standards, emphasizing hygienic and infection-free blood transfusion. Each unit of blood and blood component is tested for HIV, Hepatitis B, Hepatitis C, Syphilis and Malaria.

Department of Radiation Oncology and Medical Physics

The Department of radiation oncology is headed by a team of Radiation Oncologists, Surgical oncologists, Medical oncologists and Medical Physicists. The aim is to provide focused and comprehensive oncology care. Dr Kamakshi Memorial Hospital believes that treating the whole person; mind, body and spirit, instead of the disease, may pay better outcome. To enable the patients to receive the most technologically advanced Radiation therapy, the hospital has brought in the state-of-the-art “Primus” Digital Linear Accelerator (Siemens) with the capability of generating dual-energy photons (6MV and 15MV X-rays) and electrons of six energies initially (2005). The latest Elekta (Synergy) Infinity linear accelerator, recently commissioned, is the advanced state of art facility with 6,10,15 & 6FFF photon energies and five electron energies, capable of delivering high precision radiotherapy techniques like SRS ( Stereotactic Radio Surgery ),SRT ( Stereotactic Radiotherapy ), SBRT ( Stereotactic Body Radiotherapy ), VMAT ( Volumetric Modulated Arc Therapy ) and DCAT ( Dynamic Conformal Arc Therapy ) along with ABC ( Active Breathing Co-ordinator) gating and KVCBCT ( Kilo voltage Cone Beam Computed tomography). 3D-Conformal Radiation therapy, intensity-modulated radiation therapy (IMRT), Image-guided radiation therapy (IGRT) has revolutionized western countries in the treatment of cancer, which is also available at the Dr. Kamakshi Memorial Group of Hospitals. The hospital also has a High Dose Rate (HDR) Brachytherapy which is another form of radiotherapy. It will provide Intracavitary, surface mould, Intraluminal and interstitial implant treatment at an affordable cost.
